About Me

I’m Kevin Kolb, a lifelong Saints fan and game creator with roots in New Orleans and a history in corporate training, software, and storytelling. I mix tech and narrative to build games that live in the real world — from Spelling Bee tools to walking tours with puzzles and payphones. My projects often blend history, humor, and a love for local culture.
